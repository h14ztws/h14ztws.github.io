About 关于我们

Welcome! We are Hangzhou High-school Astronomical Associations Union(HHAAU).

欢迎！我们是杭州高中天文联合会。

We were founded in 2014. We are a multi-school astronomy association union of high school students in Hangzhou.

我们正式成立于2014年，是一个属于高中生自己的多校天文社团联合会。

We hope to establish a more open communication platform from the perspective of high school students in order to achieve better results and progress together.

我们希望从高中生的角度去建立一个更开放的交流平台，以达到更好的效果，实现共同进步。

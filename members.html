Hangzhou No.14 High School Astronomy Association
 杭十四中天文社

Members 我们的成员

We are big fans of the starry sky, and interested in a variety of topics in astronomy.

我们对星空充满了向往，对天文学的许多方向感兴趣。

Most of us are students of Zhejiang Province Hangzhou No.14 High School.

我们的成员主要来自浙江省杭州第十四中学。

We will have a formal gathering at the beginning of each fall semester(always in September) to recruit new members. Of course, you can also apply to join us during the whole term.

我们将在每年9月开学时举行招新活动, 当然您也可以在学期中申请加入我们。

We welcome all students interested in astronomy to join us！

我们欢迎所有对天文感兴趣的同学加入我们！

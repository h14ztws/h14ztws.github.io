Observatory 天文台

Our Observatory(Fengqi Observatory) located in Hangzhou No.14 High School, Xiacheng Dist., Hangzhou, Zhejiang Province, China (30°15'59.9"N ,120°09'16.0"E,Alt=35m).

我们的天文台（凤起天文台）位于浙江省杭州第十四中学凤起校区，地理坐标：东经120°09’16.0″，北纬30°15’59.9″，海拔35米。



Fengqi Observatory was built in 20th century, and the whole observatory was completely upgraded in 2013.

凤起天文台始建于20世纪，2013年天文台整体改造升级。
